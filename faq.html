F.A.Q

General

What is this website about?

This website provides access to information about buildings, rooms, and amenities within our institution, along with account features and functionality for users and administrators.

Who can use this website?

Students, faculty, and staff with authorized login credentials can access this platform.

Account and Login

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the login page and filling in the required details.

What should I do if I forget my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.

How can I update my account information?

Once logged in, go to the "My Account" section to update your profile and other information.

Navigation and Building Information

How do I search for a specific building?

Use the search bar or select a building from the "Suggestions" list on the home page.

Can I get detailed information about a specific floor in a building?

Yes, click on a building, then select a specific level to view detailed information about its features and amenities.

What types of information are available for each building?

Information includes area, year of completion, occupancy, levels, and available amenities such as study rooms or lounges.

Contact and Support

Who should I contact for technical issues or suggestions?

Use the "Contact" or "Feedback" option in the menu to reach our support team.

Is there a guide for new users?

Yes, refer to the FAQ or reach out via the "F.A.Q" section for assistance.

How can I provide feedback about the website?

Use the "Feedback" option in the side menu to share your comments or suggestions.

Miscellaneous

Yes, we ensure all information is up-to-date, but you can report discrepancies through the "Feedback" section.

Yes, our platform is optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
